PixelzLab | Privacy Policy

17-02-2025

Effective Date: February 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PixelzLab ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile applications, including HeartX: Health Track ("Applications").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Profile information (age, gender, height)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (app features used, time spent in app)

1.2 Health Data

Our Applications may collect the following health-related data:

  • Heart Rate Data: Measurements of heart rate (BPM), heart rate variability (HRV), and related metrics
  • Blood Pressure Data: Systolic and diastolic pressure readings, pulse rate
  • Blood Sugar Data: Blood glucose level measurements
  • Weight Data: Body weight measurements

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Personal Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide and improve our Applications
  • Personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about updates or changes
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve functionality

2.2 Health Data

We use your health data to:

  • Display your health metrics within the Applications
  • Track changes in your health metrics over time
  • Provide insights and visualizations based on your data
  • Generate personalized recommendations (for informational purposes only)

Important: All health data is stored locally on your device by default. We do not transmit or store your health data on our servers unless you explicitly enable optional cloud backup features.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Health data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • If cloud backup features are enabled, data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure data protection

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information or health data to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who help us operate our Applications (e.g., cloud storage providers, analytics services)
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot reasonably be used to identify you

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Export: You can access and export your health data through the Application settings
  • Deletion: You can delete your health data at any time through the Application
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of optional data collection features
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the Applications will remove all locally stored data

6. Data Retention

We retain your health data for as long as you use the Applications or until you delete the data. If you enable cloud backup features, we retain your data until you delete it or request its removal.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Applications are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from your child, please contact us.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Our Applications are designed for informational and educational purposes only. They are not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The Applications are not medical devices and should not be used as substitutes for professional medical care. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals regarding your health.

9. International Users

Our Applications are operated from the United States. If you are located outside of the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Applications, you consent to this transfer and processing of your data.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at itmanz.com@gmail.com.
